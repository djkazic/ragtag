RAGTAG

ENTERPRISE GRADE SOPHISTICATION. STUDENT LEVEL EASE.

Tools that let you see the big picture are often daunting to use, setup, and maintain. Why should that be the case? Computing can be more accessible, and RagTag is how we aim to achieve that.

What is RagTag?

Ragtag is a parallel task manager and dynamic controller developed on the Java platform. Define a binary that matches our easy-to-meet requirements and click a button. A built-in HTTP server lets you keep track of jobs and workers through a simple and minimalist interface, while a REST API makes extensibilty a snap. Worried about distributing your binary? Ragtag streams it over the network to your nodes, with its binary caching technology keeping communications efficient and streamlined.

What can I use it for?

Whether you're a small business owner with a few spare PC's, or a college student with limited hardware -- RagTag enables your ability to experience, experiment, and learn distributed computing practices and development. This is the democratization of cluster computing.
